American Curl

Temperamento: Affectionate, Curious, Intelligent, Interactive, Lively, Playful, Social

Años de vida: 12 - 16

Descripción: Distinguished by truly unique ears that curl back in a graceful arc, offering an alert, perky, happily surprised expression, they cause people to break out into a big smile when viewing their first Curl. Curls are very people-oriented, faithful, affectionate soulmates, adjusting remarkably fast to other pets, children, and new situations.

Amigable con niños: 4

Amigable con perros: 5

Origen: United States
